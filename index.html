About Us

Masaya Event Organizer is a unit of business in the event management sector under PT. Bumi Daya Plaza. Masaya EO supported by a skillful team and versed in event management both in the scale of national and international. With determination and creativity, Masaya EO intends to help grow the industry in Indonesia to become capable of developing and become the greatest and have an impact on the country's economic growth.

Vision

Become the best in Event Management business and to be able to compete on the international level.

Mision


Provide optimal benefits for all stakeholders

Create and Promote best event in the Industry

Open employment opportunities to support national development
